About Me

Welcome to the online portfolio website of Sharice "MindMistress" Croft - Artist and front-end web developer/coder by hobby.

  • Real Name: Sharice Croft
  • Age: 32
  • Pronouns: They/Them/She/Her
  • Occupation: Web Developer/Digital and Sketch Artist

Whether it's online webpages, graphic design, stories, or illustrations, KGDigital is my all-in-one convenient hub website solely dedicated to showcasing all of my artwork and personal projects through all of the years until now and beyond.



This personal web-page is also a beginning stage of what has long been a dream of mine, which is to effectively lay the groundwork for what I envision as a Creators' Guild; a decentralized network of countless different individual websites among various self-taught artists of a diverse range of backgrounds, ethnicities, genders, neuro-divergence, and cultures as well as among a variety of fields and disciplines where pure, unrestrained creativity can thrive without the taint of corporate profit motive, patriarchal/heteronormative/white supremacist/ablelist hierarchy, or outside pressure. An environment for those of us who spend every waking moment of our days and nights creating, experimenting, and pushing our limits, tackling any project we can think of, pursuing any idea we can dream of, and searching for others to share with and can take it even further; others whom, like us, have that strong sense of joy, determination, will, audacity, strength, compassion, and adventure, a healthy level of insanity, and a genuine desire to help the world...just because we can!
